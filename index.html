Vote NOW!










Why?

Our population has gotten increasingly misinformed regarding the voting process, and the voter turnout rate has been going down and down. We need to do something lest our democracy dissolves.

How can I help?

Just vote! Our democracy relies on the participation of it's constituents. In order to shape the future of America, we the people must vote and represent our own views, contributing and creating an America that encapsulates---hollistically---all our disparate views.
